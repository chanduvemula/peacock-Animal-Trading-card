Peacock

The peacock tail contains spectacular beauty because of the large feathers, bright, iridescent colours and intricate patterns.

  • Scientific Name: Pavo cristatus
  • Diet: omnivorous
  • Average Lifespan: 15 years
  • Habitat: Rainforest

The Peacock is found in many locations including Burma, Indian, and Sri Lanka. They tend to live in locations that offer them access to low trees and plants.The Peacock feeds on a variety of food items. Grain is on of the most common items that they eat.The male peafowl can have up to 150 brightly colored feathers on its tail coverts.
